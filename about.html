THE VISION

Is to create a value addition, provide high quality, tailor- made customer focused diversified product line, expanding the frontiers of the oil seed processing in Africa and beyond.
Through:

Production of high quality proteins (feed grade) for companies in Ghana and the sub- region.
Production of high quality soya oil (edible grade)for companies in Ghana and the sub region.
Provision of effective support services to farmers.
Foster stronger and deeper collaboration with our partners and facilitators.
