PARTNERSHIPS

AND WE ARE HAPPY TO DO IT

Dragon Farming limited - through the assistance of USAID-ADVANCE program have facilitated meetings between farmer groups, aggregators and nucleus farmers. This is to create a link between the farmer and the end user. Such meetings have encouraged farmers to form associations and better improve their relationships with off-takers with great success.

Dragon Farming Ltd every year participates in PRE-HARVEST. One of the biggest agribusiness events in Ghana organised by USAID-ADVANCE and GRAINS COUNCIL (GGC). The event allows for direct interactions between all the actors in the grains value chain. It is also a platform to share ideas and also complete supply deals. Dragon Farming Ltd have taken full advantage of this platform and have increase the number of actors in our supply chain. This have made it easier for us to source our raw materials for production locally.

We also have regular trade mission to major soya producing areas in the northern regions. This is mostly facilitated by USAID-ADVANCE II. This trade missions allow us to have interactions with the farmers and the community leaders on ways to foster better business relationships and also improve production. Such missions are reassuring to our out growers and it also increases their confidence in holding fast to the farming business. Dragon Farming Ltd has been criss-crossing the length and breath of Ghana paying working visit to farmers in the northern region where most of the raw materials for our factory are produced. We have always been received warmly from the farmers. We have in turn shown our appreciation by continually improving our working relationship with our nucleus farmers and aggregators.

USAID- ADVANCE II its office in Bolgatanga in the Upper East Region facilitated a working visit to farmer based organisations in that part of the country. This was an eye opener for our organisation. We were surprised to know and experience how extensive the Inter-Grow brand has become a household name and so common with our nucleus farmers and aggregators.
