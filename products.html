Vegetable Soy Oil

Our refined soy oil is the taste of many homes because of the finest process it undergoes. To produce our Golden soybean oil, the soybeans are cracked, adjusted for moisture content, heated to between 60 and 88 °C (140–190 °F), rolled into flakes, and solvent-extracted with hexanes. The oil is then refined through our time tested processes to obtain our healthy Golden soy oil.







Soya Meal

Our freshly pressed cake can further be grinded into a Fresh, crispy meal with our 4tonne/hr hammer mill, sealed in a polythene bag fresh. This is done to eliminate contamination and improve product quality. Our product has a protein concentration of 45.5%, stable for over twelve months and maintaining between 4-8% of oil content. It is recommended for fish feed producers, poultry feed producers, and industries looking for a protein base as a carrier.

Full Fat Soy Oil

When soy beans are heat treated without the removal of oil the product is called Full Fat Soy Bean Meal. This soy product is more digestible and is more superior product when compared to other forms of soy. Our Top grade full fat soybean meal is obtained by first cooking or roasting the soybeans. We further micronize the beans then to our specialize machines to extrude the meal, using control methods to manage the temperate and other factors. Full fat soybean contains high fat levels and is a good source of all the essential amino acids for livestock’s. The heating in the cooking is required to destroy the anti-nutritional factor, trypsin inhibitors.
